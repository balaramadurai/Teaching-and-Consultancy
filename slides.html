4 Basic Premise

Ideas implemented should lead to sales/revenue from the customer

An effective idea in the context of a company or a startup is the one that actually makes money for the company or startup. This can be done via the creation of a product or a service. This is often referred to as innovation as well. The creation of the idea is often referred to as invention or problem solving.

A Four Step Efficient and Systematic Process called Karmic Design Thinking

These four stages of design thinking are achieved in sequence or in iteration of all these steps.

  1. People thru Empathy - The first step in getting to know or empathize with customer's pain points is to either go through the experience yourself or closely observe people while they are going through their experience. You can make many observations about how they go about whatever it is that they going through. For example, if they are riding a bicycle, your company's bicycle, then you can ride the bicycle yourself and observe. Additionally, you can ask many customers to mount the bicycle for you and you can make observations on how they are doing it. This step will allow you to get inside the heart of a customer.

"The Disaster"

Meet Curio, the Student

  • 22 years old, Reputed University
  • She goes for an internship at Reputed Inc.

7 Episode 2 - Empathize This

"There is suffering in the world"

Curio looked for suffering

She came across a kid playing with a mobile (made by Reputed Inc)

Kid played with her mobile all of her spare time

Gesid's voice warned Curio not to believe the one kid's behaviour

Curio observed and interviewed a number of kids

Curio arrived at the conclusion that kids spend considerable spare time on the mobile

Gesid's voice echoed to draw out the story from the kid's perspective
